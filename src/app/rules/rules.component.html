Knights of Node is an exciting game where you defend the honor of the one true JavaScript framework, Angular.

How to play the game: The player starts in the center of the screen. Enemies spawn randomly throughout the screen. There are two types of enemies; a black dragon and a red dragon. There's no way out. Kill all the dragons and save yourself or die. Have fun!

Characters

Brad the Valiant Angular Knight

Brad tirelessly roams the countryside in order to rid the Land of Radian of Jordan the Dragon. He only wishes people peace and long life. He uses his sword to fight bugs, cut scope creep, open every door to sacred truth of source code. He uses his mighty shield to protect the denizens, so that they can share their code, collaborate on Slack, and pass their unit tests.

Jordan the Dragon

Jordan the Dragon, with his massive talons, fiery breath, and undefined objects terrorizes the Land of Radian. He wears the Amulet of React, which allows him to Object.assign({{ '{}' }}, this) himself. The swarms of dragons move across the country side devouring the denizens, open source software, and passing e2e tests. Continuous integrations fail. Backends are filled with compromised data. JavaScript consoles are flooded with errors from the work of his evil drones.

Occasionally, the Amulet of React throws a TypeError, which creates a Super Jordan so enraged his skin turns red and all code is deleted using srm taking all backups and repositories with it. This leads Jordan on a mindless rampage across the Land of Radian.

Vitalik Vitality Tokens

A gift from the crypto Gods! Vitalik Vitality tokens are committed to the blockchain so that Brad may continue on his noble quest to defend the Land of Radian and eradicate Jordan from the countryside, so the denizens can commit and push their changes and make their deadlines.
